LIQUID SEASONINGS

Liquid Seasonings are natural blends of herbs and spice extracts which provide our customers with a complex, authentic flavour profile suitable for many applications. These are convenient products for the food industry with a strong profile compared to the raw powder blends. Compared to the powder blends, liquid blends have a long shelf life and negligible moisture content.

Application

• Seasonings
• Savoury & Culinary
• Processed Meat (Meat Products, Minced Meat, and Sausages etc.)
• Sauces, Salad Dressings & Gravies
• Beverages
• Confectioneries
• Fortification Of Powdered Spice Blends
• Other Processed Foods
