ENCAPSULATED & SPRAYDRIED

Encapsulation describes a process of capturing active compounds of tastes in a more stable form of protective flavour pockets. Our encapsulated and spray dried range is carefully processed to retain the vitality of the plants and herbs. This allows for a controlled release of flavours at the right time and enhances the shelf life of your product.
