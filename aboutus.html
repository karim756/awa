ABOUT GROUP

Eng. Wael Ghandour

Founder of awa Group

Eng. Wael Ghandour established AWA Group and as a subsidiary, formed AWA Food Additives, AWA Food Solutions and AWA Ingredients Supply and the latest addition, AWA Bio Ingredients. A chemical engineer by profession, Ghandour is a true visionary at every sense of the word and loves to explore and experiment. Always in search of new ideas and concepts, in the heart of hearts, he is an avid traveller on the lookout for 'the' cutting edge concept.
